About me

Welcome to my blog! My name is Daniella, but you can call me Dani or Zam if you prefer. I'm a born and raised Wellingtonian, so naturally some things I enjoy are coffee and food. I love to cook, create, eat, nibble, really anything about it. Other things I love (in no particular order) are dogs, hot sauce, cryptic crosswords, roller skating and going on adventures.

I recently found myself in an awkward situation. I'd been working full time for over 10 years since finishing high school. I found myself at a bit of a loose end, having been made redundant from my job and through the slog of applying and interviewing for a new job (in admin mostly) I discovered that I REALLY didn't even want a new job in admin, which is ultimately what drove me to find something new and interesting to do and how I eneded up at EDA.

The blog posts in my site are all blogs published as part of doing phase-0 of Enspiral Dev Academy.
