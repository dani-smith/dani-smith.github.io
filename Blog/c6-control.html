Emotional Triggers

I am a somewhat high-stress person in a lot of ways and stress can very easily trigger an emotional response for myself. When coding I think the stress of not being able to work something out within what I think of as a reasonable amount of time definitely triggers an unwarranted emotional response. This response generally comes in the form of a verbal outburst and a desire to quit what I am doing.
